How It Works

1. Pick a side: Boy or Girl.

2. Enter your bet amount (minimum $20). All bets go partly towards baby necessities.

3. Half of the total bets will be used for baby necessities; the other half is divided proportionally among those who guessed correctly.

4. Optional Birth Date Guess (+$5): If you guess the exact date and the correct gender, you get Pi Shnayim (Double Portion). That means you don’t just get one share of the winners’ pot—you get two shares.

5. Example of Potential Winnings:

- Suppose at the end we collect $2,000 total. - $1,000 goes toward baby necessities, $1,000 for the winners.
- If the winning side collectively bet $400, and you personally bet $50, that’s 12.5% of the winning side’s total. So you get 12.5% of $1,000 = $125. - If you also guessed the date correctly (Pi Shnayim), you get an *additional* portion, so your total is $250!

6. Bets close on February 1, 2025 at 11:59 PM. Good luck and have fun!
